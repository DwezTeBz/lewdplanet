The story begins as the MC arrives at his aunt's house in America, where he recently moved in from Japan, where his family lives at the moment.

He lives with his aunt and cousins, while his uncle is on a business trip.

You have the possibility to follow different paths when you interact with people, depending on your actions and answers, the story will be different.

The game is currently under development and a lot of content is planned in updates that will depend on personal funding. Ideally, we would like to release the finished version by July 2023.
